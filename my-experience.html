MY EXPERIENCE

My unforgettable experience is while I represent my school in the futsal competition at Kuala Muda district level.We worked hard,practiced day and night to prepare for the competition.On the day of the competition I was so nervous and wondered if we could win the competition. But the thanks to hardwork and high team spirit, we managed to win the futsal match and what is most gratifying is that i scored the winning goal in the final match. What have i learned from this experience is hardwork and effort is the way to gain success.
